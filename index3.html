My Hobby's

Whether I’m gliding through the water for exercise or just relaxing in a pool, swimming brings me a sense of peace and vitality. It’s a fantastic way to stay fit while enjoying the refreshing feel of water. Another passion of mine is drawing. I love capturing moments, emotions, and ideas on paper. It allows me to express my creativity and explore different styles and techniques. Drawing serves as both a creative outlet and a way to unwind after a busy day.

I also take great pride in my participation in Model United Nations. This experience not only enhances my public speaking and debating skills but also deepens my understanding of global issues. Engaging in MUN has taught me the importance of diplomacy and collaboration, skills that are invaluable in today’s interconnected world. Cooking is another hobby I cherish.

enjoy experimenting with new recipes and ingredients, creating delicious meals for my family and friends.
